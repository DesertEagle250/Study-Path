Day 1

1. Understand The Assignment
- Read all the instructions carefully. Identify the types of problems (e.g., algebra, geometry, calculus) and concepts involved.

2. Brake the Work into Chunks
- Group similar problems together (e.g., all linear equations, then all quadratic equations).
- Repair notes for each segment.

3. Problem-Solving Session
- Start with the most challenging sections to tackle them with a fresh mind.
- Use Pomodoro Technique (study in 25-minute focused sessions with 5-minute breaks) to maintain concentration.

4. Review
- Go over your completed tasks to correct mistakes.
- Write notes on how to avoid them in the future.

Suggestion: Use the “Pomodoro Technique” in order to maximize your effectiveness.
