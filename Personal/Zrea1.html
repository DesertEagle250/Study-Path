Day 1

1. Initial Assessment
- Skim through the assignment to grasp the main topics and structure.

2. Organization
- Define clear goals for what you need to understand and retain.
- Divide the reading into manageable sections with specific time allocations.

3. Focused Reading Sessions
- Use Active Reading(engage with the text by highlighting key points and making notes) in order to deepen the understanding.
- After each section, write a brief summary to reinforce understanding.
- Formulate questions about the content and answer them, to deepen comprehension.

4. Review and Reflect
- Use Concept Mapping(create visual representations of the main ideas and their connections) to fully grasp the idea behind the text.
- If possible, discuss the material with a peer to gain different perspectives.

Suggestion: Use the “Pomodoro Technique” in order to maximize your effectiveness.
