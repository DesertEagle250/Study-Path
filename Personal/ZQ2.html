2-Day Plan

Day 1

1. Topic Review
- Brake the material into smaller topics.
- Highlight important concepts, formulas, or definitions.
- Divide topics into chunks and review each. Use Mind Mapping to visualize connections between topics.

2. Review (Active Recall)
- Revise those notes and try to remember as much as you can
- Put them away
- Write down everything you can remember from those notes
- Check the result with your notes and see what you forgot
- Repeat until you remember everything
- Use Mnemonics (memory tricks that best suit your topic) for information that were challenging for you

Day 2

3. Revision 2
- Revise your notes you studied/made on Day 1 using the Feynman Technique
(Teach the material out loud or write it down as if explaining it to a 5-year-old. If you struggle, revisit your notes to fill in the gaps).

4. Practice Tests
- Test yourself on the material using flashcards or practice questions.
-If you don’t have any practice questions you can use AI (for example ChatGPT) to quiz you.

5. Final Review
- Go over your notes for the last time.
- Revise those parts, on which you have failed or had difficulties on the practice tests.
-Use Mnemonics (memory tricks that best suit your topic) for information that were challenging for you

Suggestion: Use the “Pomodoro Technique” in order to maximize your effectiveness.
