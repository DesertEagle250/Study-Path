2-Day Plan

Day 1

1. Topic Review
- Brake the topic from which you will be writing the exam into smaller topics

2. Research
- Gather information using credible sources.
- Summarize your research, via notes.

3. Brainstorming
- Brainstorm and write down possible topics for of exam.

Day 2

4.Revision 1 (Active Recall)
- Revise your notes and try to remember as much as you can.
- Put them away.
- Write down everything you can remember from those notes.
- Check the result with your notes and see what you forgot.
- Repeat until you remember everything.
- Use Mnemonics (memory tricks that best suit your topic) for information that were challenging for you

5.Revision 2 (Feynman Technique)
- Try to answer all the previously noted possible topics of you exams using the Feynman Technique
(explain those topics out loud as if you're teaching someone unfamiliar with the topic)this ensure a deep understanding of the topic and helps you answer such questions fluently.

6.Revision 3
- Revise how to write an essay.

Suggestion: Use the “Pomodoro Technique” in order to maximize your effectiveness.
