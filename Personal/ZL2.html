2-Day Plan

Consider applying Mindfulness Techniques to your studies.

Click here to find out more about Mindfulness

Day 1

1. Understand The Assignment
- Read the instructions carefully to identify key tasks.
- Use Elaborative Interrogation (ask yourself “Why is this task important?” and “How does it help me improve my language skills?”) to stay motivated.

2. Brake the tasks down
- Divide the homework into smaller sections, like vocabulary, grammar, reading, and writing tasks.

3. Solving
- Start with the most challenging sections to tackle them with a fresh mind.
- Start with revising notes from lessons.
- If you don’t have said notes make them using handbooks, or you friends notes.
- Use Pomodoro Technique (study in 25-minute focused sessions with 5-minute breaks) to maintain concentration.
- Work until you feel burned-out, as to ensure the quality of the homework.

Day 2


4. Solving 2
- Finish the rest of the homework.
- Start with revising notes from lessons.
- Use Pomodoro Technique (study in 25-minute focused sessions with 5-minute breaks) to maintain concentration.

5. Review
- Go over your completed tasks to correct mistakes.
- Write notes on how to avoid them in the future.

Suggestion: Use the “Pomodoro Technique” in order to maximize your effectiveness.
