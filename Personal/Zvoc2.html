2-Day Plan

Consider applying Mindfulness Techniques to your studies.

Click here to find out more about Mindfulness

Day 1

1. Assesment
- Identify which words are unfamiliar.

2. Notes
- Create flashcards from the syllabus.
- Use Dual Coding (incorporate drawings and pictures into your flashcards) to reinforce understanding.

3. Revision
- Use The Pomodoro Technique to stay productive (break your work into 25-minute focused sessions with 5-minute breaks).
- Repeat until you remember everything.
- Use Mnemonics (memory tricks that best suit your topic)
for information that were challenging for you. Write sentences or a short story using the new vocabulary to understand usage.
- Mark words that are hard to remember.

4. Application
- Write sentences or a short story using the new vocabulary to understand usage.

Day 2


5. Revision
- Revise words covered during day 1.
- Revise the rest of the words.
- Put emphasizes on words that are challenging for you.
- Use The Pomodoro Technique to stay productive (break your work into 25-minute focused sessions with 5-minute breaks).
- Repeat until you remember everything.
- Use Mnemonics (memory tricks that best suit your topic)
for information that were challenging for you. Write sentences or a short story using the new vocabulary to understand usage.

5. Application
- Write sentences or a short story using the new vocabulary to understand usage.

6. Final Review
- Go through the flashcards once more, focusing on troublesome words.

Suggestion: Use the “Pomodoro Technique” in order to maximize your effectiveness.
